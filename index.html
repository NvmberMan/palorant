Combine style and experience in the global competitive scene. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side with intense shooting skills and tactical abilities.

With only one life per round, you must think faster than your opponents to survive. Take down enemies in Competitive, Unranked, Deathmatch, and Spike Rush modes.
